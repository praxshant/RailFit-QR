Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I reset my account password?

Contact your system administrator or use the password reset option during login. For demo accounts, use the provided credentials.

What image formats are supported for QR scanning?

The system supports JPG, PNG, TIFF, and BMP formats. For best results, use high-resolution images with good lighting.

How do I integrate with UDM/TMS portals?

Integration credentials are managed by your system administrator. Contact technical support for API access and configuration.

Can I access the system on mobile devices?

Yes, the system is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices. Live QR scanning works best on devices with good cameras.
